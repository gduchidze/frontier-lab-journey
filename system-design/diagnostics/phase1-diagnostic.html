Phase 1 Gap Detector

180 questions — 20 per lesson topic. No notes, no peeking — wrong answers here are pure profit: they tell us exactly where to dig.

How to use (ADHD-fit) One topic block (20 questions) = one sitting. Progress saves automatically — close the tab anytime. Each topic's verdict appears the moment its 20th answer lands: GAP (<50% — full deep-dive session), SHAKY (50–79% — redo the lab + targeted review), STRONG (≥80% — spaced recall only). After each sitting, copy the summary block and paste it to your teacher — that becomes your deep-dive plan.

Retake after the deep-dives — the delta between attempts is the progress measure. Questions rotate meaning across retakes poorly, so the second attempt is a floor check, not a victory lap.
