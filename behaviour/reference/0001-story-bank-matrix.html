Reference · Story Bank

Story Bank Matrix

8–10 true stories × 6 competencies. Bullets only — a scripted story is a dead story. Print this, fill by hand, keep it beside you in every mock.

The rules

  • Bullets, never scripts. 4–6 bullet points per story: context (1), stakes (1), your actions (2–3), measured result + what you learned (1). Recalling bullets sounds like thinking; recalling scripts sounds like reciting — an explicit failure mode at Anthropic.
  • Each story covers ≥3 competencies. A 4–6 round loop burns questions fast; stories must be reusable from different angles without repeating.
  • Senior bar: the Result row must show organizational impact — what changed about how the team/company works — not just "the bug got fixed".
  • Quantify. Latency numbers, revenue, headcount influenced, incidents prevented. Specific metrics are the strongest authenticity signal.

Per-story bullet template

• Result: <metric> + changed: <what the org does differently now>
