# Behavioral Rounds Resources

## Knowledge

- [Guide: Anthropic Culture Interview — IGotAnOffer](https://igotanoffer.com/en/advice/anthropic-culture-interview)
  Round structure and reported questions for the 45-min culture interview. Use for: understanding what "mission-first, high-trust/low-ego" is tested as, not just stated as.
- [Guide: Anthropic Behavioral Interview 2026 — Preper](https://preper.app/guides/anthropic-behavioral-interview-prep)
  Flags the key failure mode: performing values instead of demonstrating them; rewards honest skepticism. Use for: calibrating tone before Anthropic mocks.
- [Question bank: OpenAI Behavioral — Exponent](https://www.tryexponent.com/questions?company=openai&role=swe&type=behavioral)
  Reported OpenAI behavioral questions. Use for: drill rotation, ownership/ambiguity themes.
- [Question bank: Google DeepMind — Exponent](https://www.tryexponent.com/questions?company=google-deep-mind)
  Reported DeepMind questions incl. culture round. Use for: teamwork/feedback/research-collaboration themes.
- [Guide: The STAR Method complete guide — The Interview Guys](https://blog.theinterviewguys.com/the-star-method/)
  Baseline mechanics: Situation/Task/Action/Result, Action ≈ 50% of airtime. Use for: story structure at OpenAI/DeepMind (NOT verbatim at Anthropic culture round).
- [Guide: Master the Behavioral Interview as a Senior Engineer — Shine Garg (Maven)](https://maven.com/uncharted-path-breakthroughs/senior-engineer-behavioral-interviews)
  Senior-bar framing: organizational impact and influence, not personal delivery. Use for: upgrading stories from "I fixed X" to "I changed how the team ships".
- Primary sources for lab literacy (read the labs' own words, not summaries):
  - [Anthropic: Core Views on AI Safety](https://www.anthropic.com/news/core-views-on-ai-safety) — the worldview the culture round probes against
  - [OpenAI Charter](https://openai.com/charter/) — AGI-benefit framing OpenAI expects you to have engaged with
  - [Google DeepMind: About](https://deepmind.google/about/) — mission language for the culture fit round
